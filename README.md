## üìñ Overview

**EduSafe Connect** is a secure, digital reporting platform designed to create safer educational environments. It acts as a confidential bridge connecting students and parents directly with the Ministry of Education and Child Services. By leveraging technology, we ensure every voice is heard while implementing intelligent systems to filter data and prioritize genuine emergencies.

---

## üö© The Problem

In the current educational landscape, three critical issues persist:

1.  **Silence due to Fear:** Students and parents often fear victimization or backlash if they report bullying, abuse, or negligence through traditional, non-anonymous channels.
2.  **Information Gap:** Authorities often lack real-time, data-driven insights into what is happening on the ground in schools.
3.  **Resource Drain:** Traditional reporting lines are often clogged with vague or false reports, making it difficult for case workers to identify and act on serious threats quickly.

---

## üí° The Solution

EduSafe Connect solves these problems through a three-pronged approach:

1.  **Guaranteed Anonymity:** A secure platform that allows users to report incidents without revealing their identity to their peers or school administration.
2.  **Intelligent Triage:** Utilizing AI-driven logic to categorize and prioritize reports, ensuring that urgent safety concerns are flagged immediately.
3.  **Holistic Support:** Moving beyond just "reporting" by providing immediate mental health resources, legal aid directories, and a community support forum.

---

## üåç Alignment with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

## üîÆ Upcoming Features

To further robust the platform and reduce misuse, the following features are in development:

* **AI Credibility Scoring:** A backend machine-learning model that analyzes report text and evidence to assign a "Credibility Score," helping case workers distinguish between high-priority threats and potential spam.
* **Voice-to-Text Reporting:** Accessibility features allowing younger students or those with disabilities to record their reports via audio, which is then transcribed by AI.
* **Multi-Language Support:** Native support for Swahili and other local dialects to ensure inclusivity across all regions.
* **Corroboration System:** Allowing multiple users to securely and anonymously "back up" a specific report (e.g., witnessing the same bullying incident), adding weight to the case.
