Why EduSafe Connect?

Confidential & Secure

Your identity and report details are protected with advanced security measures, ensuring peace of mind when you speak up.

Direct to Authorities

Reports are routed directly to the relevant child services or Ministry of Education officials for prompt and appropriate action.

Focus on Verification

Our intelligent system helps prioritize credible reports, reducing the risk of false submissions and ensuring effective resource allocation.

How It Works

1. Submit Your Report

Fill out our secure, detailed form with all the necessary information and any evidence you may have. Be factual and specific.

2. Receive a Case ID

Upon submission, you will instantly receive a unique and confidential Case ID. Keep this safe to track your report's progress.

3. Official Review & Action

Your report is confidentially reviewed by the appropriate authorities, who then initiate verification and take necessary steps.

4. Track Progress Online

Use your Case ID on our tracking page to view real-time status updates, offering transparency throughout the process.
